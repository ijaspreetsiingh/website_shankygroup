Who We Are

What is Shanky Group?

Shanky Group is a distinguished and rapidly growing business conglomerate headquartered in Delhi, India. With a robust presence across diverse sectors, the Group has established itself as a dynamic force in financial services, food and agribusiness, solar EPC and electronics, education and corporate training, infrastructure and construction, and metals trading.

The Group's portfolio comprises seven core companies—each a leader in its respective domain—united by a shared commitment to operational excellence, innovation, and sustainable growth. Founded and steered by visionary leaders, Shanky Group has evolved into a multi-faceted enterprise that leverages sectoral synergies to deliver superior value to clients, partners, and stakeholders.

The Group's companies are registered with the Ministry of Corporate Affairs, Government of India, and adhere to the highest standards of corporate governance and compliance. Operational headquarters are located at D-Mall, Netaji Subhash Place, Pitampura, North West Delhi.
