About Shanky Smart Tech

Shanky Smart Tech Pvt Ltd is a group company under Shanky Group, operating at the intersection of renewable energy and electronics. We offer end-to-end solutions in solar engineering, procurement, and construction (EPC), as well as trading and servicing of electronic products for industrial and commercial clients.

Our offerings include solar panel installation, energy management systems, and smart technology integration and construction. With an authorized share capital of ₹5 lakh but ready to infused 500+ Cr , the company is poised for rapid growth in the burgeoning solar and electronics markets as well as Construction.

Shanky Smart Tech leverages cutting-edge technologies such as IoT, AI, and digital twins to enhance project efficiency, sustainability, and cost-effectiveness. Our focus on green building certifications, renewable energy integration, and smart construction practices positions us as a leader in sustainable EPC solutions Construction Work.
